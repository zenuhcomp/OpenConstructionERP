Concept paper 2026-05-25 · ~8 min read

An open construction ERP for teams who want to own their stack.

Estimating, CAD/BIM take-off, 4D/5D scheduling, procurement and site billing — in one self-hosted system under AGPL-3.0. 116 modules in v5.2.0, 55,000+ cost items across 11 regions, 57 validation rules over 16 rule sets. The architecture, how a real project flows, what it replaces and complements, the five-year TCO, and who already works with our open tools.
