Concept paper 2026-05-25 · ~8 min read

An open construction ERP for teams who want to own their stack.

Estimating, BIM take-off, 4D/5D scheduling, procurement and site billing — in one self-hosted system under AGPL-3.0. 111 modules, CWICR cost data across 11 regions, 42 validation rules, 27 UI languages. The architecture, the 16 platform areas, the five-year TCO, and who already works with our open tools.
