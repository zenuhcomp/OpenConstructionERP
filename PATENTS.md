# Patent Non-Assertion Policy

**Version 1.0 — Effective April 2026**

Artem Boiko / DataDrivenConstruction.io ("Licensor") makes the
following unilateral patent non-assertion commitment in respect
of the OpenConstructionERP project.

## 1. Definitions

- **"Software"** means the OpenConstructionERP source code,
  modules, workflows, Docker images, and official distribution
  artefacts under any of its licences
  (AGPL-3.0-or-later for the open-source edition; the
  DataDrivenConstruction commercial licence for customers who
  elect that path - see [LICENSE](./LICENSE),
  [COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md](./COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md), and
  [NOTICE](./NOTICE)).
- **"Patent Claim"** means any claim of a patent now owned,
  controlled, or licensable by Licensor anywhere in the world.

## 2. Non-Assertion Commitment

Licensor covenants not to assert any Patent Claim against any
person (the "User") using the Software, provided that:

(a) the Patent Claim reads on functionality of the Software as
    distributed by Licensor;
(b) the User's use is consistent with the applicable licence
    (AGPL-3.0-or-later or an executed commercial licence).

This commitment runs with the Software: it benefits every User,
including downstream recipients who obtain the Software from a
third party, as long as the Software is the version as
distributed by Licensor (or a modification that continues to
implement the patented functionality substantially as
distributed).

## 3. Termination for Patent Aggression

The commitment in Section 2 terminates automatically and
immediately, with respect to the aggressor and any entity under
common control with the aggressor, if that person initiates
patent litigation (including cross-claims and counter-claims)
against Licensor, against the Software, or against any
contributor alleging that the Software or a contribution to
the Software infringes a patent.

This mirrors the termination clause in Apache License 2.0 §3
and the defensive-termination mechanism of AGPL-3.0 §10.

## 4. Relationship to CLA

Contributors to OpenConstructionERP execute the Contributor
Licence Agreement (see [CLA.md](./CLA.md)), Section 3 of which
grants Licensor a royalty-free, worldwide, irrevocable patent
licence to their contributions. That grant flows to all
recipients of the Software distributed by Licensor. Combined
with this unilateral non-assertion policy, the project operates
under a broad implicit patent peace for functionality as
distributed.

## 5. Third-Party Patents

This policy covers patents owned or controlled by Licensor
only. Patents held by upstream dependencies (FastAPI,
SQLAlchemy, React, Vite, AG Grid, and other components listed
in [NOTICE](./NOTICE) and in the generated
`THIRD_PARTY_LICENSES.md`) are governed by their respective
licences.

Patents held by AI / LLM providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google,
Mistral, Groq, DeepSeek) in respect of their hosted APIs are
governed by each provider's terms of service. The Software does
not grant, and does not purport to grant, any licence under
those third-party patents.

## 6. No Waiver of Trademark Rights

This policy covers patents only. It does not licence any
trademark. See [TRADEMARK.md](./TRADEMARK.md) for the trademark
policy covering OpenConstructionERP, OpenEstimate,
DataDrivenConstruction, CWICR, and related marks.

## 7. Future Assignment

If Licensor transfers ownership of this project to a successor
legal entity - including a German Unternehmergesellschaft
(haftungsbeschränkt) (UG) or a Gesellschaft mit beschränkter
Haftung (GmbH) formed by Artem Boiko - the successor is bound
by this policy on the same terms as a condition of the transfer.
Users of the Software acquire no lesser rights as a result of
such assignment.
