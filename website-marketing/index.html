From BOQ editing and AI takeoff to 4D scheduling and tendering — everything your team needs in one platform. No more juggling spreadsheets and disconnected tools. Explore the documentation to see how each feature works.

Block-Based BOQ Editor

Build detailed bills of quantities with a spreadsheet-like editor. Create reusable assemblies, edit inline with keyboard shortcuts, and see totals update in real-time. Powered by AG Grid for handling thousands of positions smoothly.

AssembliesInline EditKeyboard ShortcutsReal-time TotalsMulti-format Export

AI-Powered Takeoff NEW

Upload a PDF floor plan or photo, and our AI automatically detects walls, doors, windows, and dimensions. Every suggestion comes with a confidence score — you review and approve before anything is added to your estimate. Items below 90% confidence are flagged for manual verification — keeping your estimates reliable.

55,000+ Cost Items

Access the CWICR (Construction Work Items, Costs & Resources) database with 55,000+ cost items covering all construction trades. Prices include detailed resource breakdowns — materials, labor, equipment, and energy. Smart search finds the right item in seconds.

Multi-CAD Import

Import drawings from AutoCAD (DWG), MicroStation (DGN), Revit (RVT), and IFC. Quantities are automatically extracted from 3D geometry — no manual measurement needed.

Multi-CAD EngineHigh Performance3D Viewer

Automatically check every BOQ position against international standards like DIN 276, NRM, MasterFormat, or GAEB. 42 built-in validation rules catch errors before they reach the client.

Enterprise Ready

Security you can trust

Self-Hosted
Deploy on your own infrastructure — cloud or on-premises. Your project data, cost databases, and client information never leave your network.
Encryption
All data encrypted with TLS 1.3 in transit and AES-256 at rest. Authentication via JWT tokens with bcrypt password hashing.
SSO & RBAC
Integrate with your existing identity provider via SAML or OIDC. Assign granular permissions with role-based access control.
Audit Log
Every edit, approval, and export is logged with timestamps and user IDs. Full audit trail for compliance and accountability.
Open Source

Built in the open,
for everyone

Licensed under AGPL-3.0 — read every line of code, audit the security yourself, extend with custom modules, and deploy on your own servers. No vendor lock-in, no hidden fees. Commercial licensing available for teams that need it. Read the documentation to get started.
